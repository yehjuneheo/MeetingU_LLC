MeetingU LLC


Independent Contractor Agreement


This Independent Contractor Agreement (this “Agreement”) is effective as of [ date ] (the “Effective Date”), between MeetingU LLC, located at Austin, Texas (the “Company”), and [ name ], located at [ location ], (the “Service Provider”), each a Party and collectively the Parties. The Service Provider desires to perform, and the Company desires to have the Service Provider perform services as an independent contractor to the Company, on the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement.


  1. Services


    1. Performance. The Service Provider will perform the services (the “Services”) described on Exhibit A to this Agreement (the “Project Description”). The Service Provider acknowledges that time is of the essence with respect to the Service Provider’s obligations to fulfill the Services hereunder and that prompt and timely performance of all such Services is strictly required.


  2. Payment


    1. Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, for the performance of the Services, the Company will pay the Service Provider the compensation as set forth in Exhibit A.

    2. Other than the consideration specified in this section, which constitutes full consideration for the Services rendered hereunder, the Service Provider will not be entitled to any other consideration for rendering the Services hereunder.


  3. Term; Termination.


    1. Term; Termination. This Agreement shall commence upon the Effective Date, and shall continue until terminated according to this Agreement.


    2. Effect of termination. The Company’s election to terminate this Agreement in accordance with its terms shall not be deemed an election of remedies, and all other remedies provided by this Agreement or available at law or in equity shall survive any termination. If the Company exercises its right to terminate this Agreement, any obligation it may otherwise have under this Agreement shall cease immediately, except that the Company shall be obligated to compensate the Service Provider for Services properly performed up to the effective date of termination. If the Service Provider exercises its right to terminate this Agreement, any obligation it may otherwise have under this Agreement shall cease immediately. Upon the expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason, the Service Provider will promptly return to the Company all Company property at the Service Provider’s expense and in accordance with the Company’s instructions. Notwithstanding any termination or expiration of this Agreement, all provisions which by their terms or sense are intended to survive will survive, including without limitation all provisions of the Confidentiality and Inventions Assignment Agreement.


  4. Relationship of the Parties

    1. Independent Contractor. The Service Provider is an independent contractor and is not an agent or employee of, and has no authority to bind, the Company by contract or otherwise. The Service Provider will perform the Services under the general direction of the Company, but the Service Provider will determine, in the Service Provider’s sole discretion, the manner and means by which the Services are accomplished, and the Company has no right or authority to control the manner or means by which the Services are accomplished, subject to the requirement that the Service Provider shall at all times comply with applicable law and the Company’s code of ethics.


    2. No Employment Taxes and Benefits. The Service Provider will report as self employment income all compensation received by the Service Provider pursuant to this Agreement. The Service Provider shall not receive any insurance or other benefits, including, without limits, workers’ compensation, unemployment, disability insurance or health benefits that are offered to employees of the Company. The Service Provider agrees to maintain its own insurance policies to cover the Service Provider’s work-related injuries (worker’s compensation) as well as general and professional liability insurance.


    3. No Right to Employment. Nothing contained in this Agreement shall be construed as giving the Service Provider and right to employment, current or future, with the Company.


    4. Use of Company Name. The Service Provider shall not make any written use of or reference to the Company’s name for any marketing, public relations, advertising, display or other business purpose or make any use of the Company’s facilities for any activity unrelated to the express business purposes and interests of the Company under this Agreement without the prior written consent of the Company, which consent may be granted or withheld in the Company’s sole discretion.

  5. Confidential Information


    As an independent contractor to the Company, the Service Provider will have access to certain Company confidential information and the Service Provider may, during the course of its engagement, develop certain information, reports, work product or analyses which will be property of the Company. As a condition to the Service Provider’s engagement as an independent contractor, the Service Provider shall sign the Company’s Confidentiality & Inventions Assignment Agreement in the form attached as Exhibit B to this Agreement concurrent with retention as an independent contractor.


  6. The Service Provider’s Assurances and Restrictive Covenants.


    The Service Provider acknowledges and recognizes the highly competitive nature of the business of the Company and its subsidiaries and affiliates and accordingly agrees as follows:


    1. Non-disparagement. The Service Provider represents, warrants and agrees that at no time, either during the Term or after the termination or expiration of thisAgreement, will the Service Provider disparage the Company or any of its products or services.


    2. Non-compete. The Service Provider agrees that, during the Term, they will promptly notify the Company in writing of any Direct Competitor of the Company to which they are also performing services. It is understood that in such an event, the Company will review the Service Provider’s activities and determine whether they shall remain as the Service Provider to the Company. “Direct Competitor” shall mean any person, entity or subsidiary of an entity that is engaged in any business that competes with the business of the company.

    3. Non-solicitation of Employees. During the Term and for 6 months after, the Parties will not, directly or indirectly, solicit or encourage to cease to work with any other Party to this agreement, or directly or indirectly hire, any person who is an employee of any other Party to this agreement then under contract with any other Party to this agreement or who was an employee of any Party to this agreement within the six (6)-month period preceding the Parties’ termination of the engagement, without the other Party’s written consent.


    4. Non-solicitation of Clients. During the Term and for 6 months after, the Service Provider will not, directly or indirectly, solicit or encourage to cease to work with the Company, any person or entity that is a client, customer, supplier, or contractor of the Company or who was a client, customer, supplier, or contractor of the Company within the six (6)-month period preceding the Service Provider’s termination of engagement without the Company’s written consent.


    5. Reasonableness of Restrictions. It is expressly understood and agreed that although the Service Provider and the Company consider the restrictions contained in this section 6 to be reasonable, if a judicial determination is made by a court of competent jurisdiction that the time or territory or any other restriction contained in this Agreement is an unenforceable restriction against the Service Provider, the provisions of this Agreement shall not be rendered void but shall be deemed amended to apply as to such maximum time and territory and to such maximum extent as such court may judicially determine or indicate to be enforceable. Alternatively, if any court of competent jurisdiction finds that any restriction contained in this Agreement is unenforceable, and such restriction cannot be amended so as to make it enforceable, such finding shall not affect the enforceability of any of the other restrictions contained herein. The provisions of this section 6 shall survive any termination or expiration of this Agreement.


    6. No Conflicts. The Service Provider has no pre-existing obligations or commitments (and will not assume or otherwise undertake any obligations or

      commitments) that would hinder the Service Provider’s performance of its obligations under this Agreement.


  7. General.


    1. Indemnification. Each Party shall indemnify the other Party and hold it harmless from and against all claims, damages, losses, and expenses, including reasonable fees and expenses of attorneys and other professionals, relating to or arising out of the indemnifying Party’s breach of any representation, warranty, or obligation under this Agreement.


    2. Complete Understanding: Modification. This Agreement, together with its exhibits, constitutes the complete and exclusive understanding and agreement of the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior understandings and agreements, whether written or oral, with respect to the subject matter hereof. In the event of a conflict, the terms and conditions of the Project Description will take precedence over the terms and conditions of this Agreement. Any waiver, modification or amendment of any provision of this Agreement will be effective only if in writing and signed by parties hereto. The waiver of any breach of any provision of this Agreement shall not constitute a waiver of any subsequent breach of the same other provisions hereof.


    3. Assignment; Subcontracting. The Service Provider shall have no right to (i) assign this Agreement, in whole or in part, by operation of law or otherwise; or (ii) subcontract or otherwise delegate the performance of the Services without the Company’s prior written consent. Any attempted assignment or delegation without such consent will be void.

      This Agreement shall be binding upon, and inure to the benefit of, the successors, executors, heirs, representatives, and permitted assigns of the parties hereto.

    4. Governing Law; Severability; Remedies. This Agreement will be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Texas; excluding its conflict of laws principles. Any legal action of proceeding arising under this Agreement will be brought exclusively in the federal or state courts located in Texas and the parties hereby consent to the exclusive in the federal or state courts located in Texas and the parties hereby consent to the exclusive personal jurisdiction and venue therein. If any provision of this Agreement is for any reason found to be unenforceable, the remainder of this Agreement will continue in full force and effect. If any action is necessary to enforce the terms of this Agreement, the substantially prevailing party will be entitled to reasonable attorney’s fees, costs, and expenses in addition to any other relief to which such prevailing party may be entitled. The parties agree that legal remedies may be inadequate to enforce the provision of this Agreement and that the Company may use equitable relief, including specific performance and injunctive relief, to enforce the provisions of this Agreement.


    5. Notices. All notices required or permitted under this Agreement will be in writing and delivered by confirmed facsimile transmission, by email with proof of receipt, by courier or overnight delivery service, or by certified mail, and in each instance will be deemed given upon receipt. All notices will be sent to the addresses set forth below or to such other addresses as may be specified by either party to the other in accordance with this section.
